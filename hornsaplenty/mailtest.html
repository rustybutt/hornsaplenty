Alameda, California

We are a Bay Area based 5 piece brass band dedicated to making beautiful music. We perform music for any occasion you might imagine - church services, wedding ceremonies, restaurant evening entertainment, parties, public events or even an afternoon concert in the park.

Our repertoire includes music for sacred holiday celebrations, Bach, classical, fanfares, 19th century Americana, wedding favorites, love songs and ballads, Dixieland and jazz.

Please take a few minutes and listen to our music and read about our players. Our band consists of three trombones and two trumpet/cornet/flugelhorns.

For indoor events, we perform without amplification and can moderate our sound level to suit most any kind of setting. And dig this... we know how to play soft!

We hope you enjoy our music and will call us for your next musical event.
